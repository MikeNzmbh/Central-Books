# Central Books — Demo Script

*Step-by-step walkthrough for demo video recording*

---

## Overview

- **Duration**: ~8-10 minutes
- **Format**: Screen recording with voiceover
- **Focus**: Show AI Companion features + code walkthrough

---

## Scene 1: Dashboard Overview (30s)

1. Open browser to `http://localhost:8000/`
2. Log in (or show logged-in state)
3. **Show**: Dashboard with Companion banner at top
4. **Narration**: "This is Central Books, an AI-powered accounting OS. The banner shows today's focus from our AI Companion."

---

## Scene 2: AI Companion Control Tower (90s)

1. Click **AI Companion** in sidebar
2. **Show the Radar** (4-axis chart)
   - "The Radar shows financial health across four dimensions: cash, revenue, expenses, and tax compliance."
3. **Show Coverage** 
   - "Coverage tells us what percentage of transactions have been reviewed by AI."
4. **Show Playbook**
   - "The Playbook surfaces prioritized actions—things I should address first."
5. **Show Story section**
   - "The Story is a weekly narrative generated by DeepSeek, summarizing what happened and what needs attention."
6. **Click an issue** to show detail (optional)

---

## Scene 3: Receipts AI (60s)

1. Navigate to **Receipts** in sidebar
2. **Upload a sample receipt image**
3. Wait for OCR processing
4. **Show extracted data**: vendor, amount, date, line items
5. **Show suggested account classification**
6. **Narration**: "GPT-4o-mini extracts the data from the image, then DeepSeek suggests how to categorize it. I approve before it posts."

---

## Scene 4: Invoices with Companion Strip (45s)

1. Navigate to **Invoices**
2. Click on an invoice (preferably overdue)
3. **Show Companion strip** with contextual insight
4. **Change status** (e.g., mark as paid)
5. **Narration**: "Each invoice has a Companion strip showing AI-generated insights. The status change updates the strip in real-time."

---

## Scene 5: Books Review (90s)

1. Navigate to **Books Review** (or AI Companion → Books Review)
2. **Set date range** (e.g., Nov 1 - Dec 10)
3. Click **Scan Books**
4. Wait for scan to complete (~30-60s)
5. **Show Deterministic Findings** section:
   - LARGE_ENTRY
   - POSSIBLE_DUPLICATE
   - OUTLIER_AMOUNT
6. **Show Neural Analysis** section:
   - AI-generated insights (if working)
   - "The deterministic engine always runs first—these are rule-based checks. The Neural Analysis adds AI reasoning on top."
7. **Show Risk Assessment** in sidebar (High Risk / Low Risk)

---

## Scene 6: Code Walkthrough - Books Review Workflow (60s)

1. Open **VS Code** with the project
2. Open `core/agentic_books_review.py`
3. **Highlight `run_books_review_workflow()` function** (Lines 32-40)
4. **Show deterministic checks** (Lines 92-108)
5. **Show conditional LLM call** (Line 126: `if ai_companion_enabled:`)
6. **Narration**: "The workflow runs deterministic checks first—always guaranteed to complete. The LLM call is optional and has a 60-second timeout."
7. **Show error handling** (Lines 237-244): "If the LLM fails, we gracefully fall back to deterministic results."

---

## Scene 7: Code Walkthrough - LLM Profiles (45s)

1. Open `companion/llm.py`
2. **Show LLMProfile enum** (Lines 63-73)
3. **Show `call_deepseek_reasoning()` function** 
4. **Highlight timeout configuration** (Lines 117-122)
5. **Narration**: "We have two profiles: HEAVY_REASONING and LIGHT_CHAT. Each surface can choose the right model for its needs. All calls have configurable timeouts."

---

## Scene 8: Code Walkthrough - Companion Control Tower (45s)

1. Open `core/views_companion.py`
2. **Show `api_companion_summary()` function** (Line 281+)
3. **Highlight**:
   - `build_companion_radar()` (Line 281)
   - `get_cached_story()` (Line 286)
   - Coverage calculation (Line 289)
   - Playbook aggregation (Line 297)
4. **Narration**: "The Control Tower aggregates data from all surfaces—radar, coverage, playbook, story. The story is pre-generated so users don't wait."

---

## Scene 9: Wrap-Up (30s)

1. Return to browser
2. Show **AI Companion** dashboard one more time
3. **Narration**: "Central Books combines deterministic accounting rules with AI-powered insights. The AI suggests, but humans approve. This is the future of small business bookkeeping."

---

## Recording Notes

- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover, minimize background noise
- **Pacing**: Pause briefly between scenes
- **VS Code tip**: Use `Cmd+G` to jump to specific line numbers
